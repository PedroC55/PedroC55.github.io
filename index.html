07
AR · PWA · Web 2025

Condeixa XR

Location-based AR Progressive Web App developed for the Municipality of Condeixa-a-Nova, offering three integrated experiences: an interactive map with georeferenced Points of Interest (MapLibre GL JS), an Augmented Reality mode where users point their device at real buildings to reveal a contextual information overlay (MindAR.js image tracking), and an embedded 360° virtual tour. All POI content is managed through a Sanity CMS headless backend, allowing the municipality to update the app without any code changes. The project pivoted from GPS-based AR (AR.js) to image tracking due to positioning inaccuracy — a real-world constraint that shaped the final architecture.
