Hi, I'm Pedro — a game developer from Aveiro, Portugal, and I've been turning ideas into playable worlds since I first realised that code could do that.

Most recently I spent a year building a gamified VR training application in Unity for Meta Quest as my Master's project in Digital Game Development — conveyor belts, socket interactions, avatar narration, the works. Before and around that: VR survival games, mixed-reality experiments that spawn lasers in your living room, AR city guides, and 360° virtual tours of wine estates.

If it's interactive, I want to build it. I care about the details players never consciously notice — the button pulse, the haptic tick, the tutorial that doesn't feel like one.

Off-screen you'll find me grinding card battlers, hunting down retro games, or planning the next trip.
